2020 GRAND MILLENIUM TOURNAMENT

The event is the fourth evolution of a championship which began in 2012. It was originally known as the Masters Grand Prix.

The 2020 Gran Millenium Tournament is the most important exhibition of annual men's tennis exhibition tournament

A week-long event, the tournament is held annually each November at the Carls Jr Complex Par in New York, United States.

This tournament feature the top eight singles players and doubles teams of the ATP Rankings.

ACES FOR BUSHFIRE RELIEF

Tennis stars from across the world are helping the victims of Australia’s bushfires by turning aces into donations.

Join the world’s best tennis players for an unforgettable night to raise funds for the victims of the bushfires.
